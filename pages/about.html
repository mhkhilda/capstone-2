My Story

Welcome! Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Hilda, aka the Greedy Learner.

I was born and raised in Hong Kong üá≠üá∞. (Yes, the ex-International Financial Center lol) Not knowing what to study, I took Quantitative Finance (some sort of Finance if you have not much understanding in all these kind of fancy stuff, it's totally understandable haha) at university. (Like many other Asian students)

Having spent roughly two years in the Financial Industry, although I enjoyed learning things that I haven't learnt before, I have absolutely no idea how they are going to benefit the society.

So one day, I came across David Graeber's Bullshit Jobs, where Graeber pushed us asking ourselves:

"What would happen were this entire class of people to simply disappear?

"Say what you like about nurses, garbage collectors, or mechanics, it's obvious that were they to vanish in a puff of smoke, the results would be immediate and catastrophic.

"A world without teachers or dockworkers would soon be in trouble, and even one without science-fiction writers or ska musicians would clearly be a lesser place."

It was then I asked myself, "What if Analysts in Financial Industry like me were to simply disappear?" "Nothing seems to happen."

I therefore started exploring things I might enjoy doing, so that I can try to develop as life-long hobbies. (Not sure if I were the only one, but I rarely have something that I genuinely like or dislike, I sometimes feel like I am just one of the robots in the factory?)

After moving to Toronto (Canada) üá®üá¶, I started devoting more time on arts related activities: ceramics, painting, doodling, crafts work, and of course web developing, all of which I genuinely enjoy a lot!

Lastly, I would like to thank all of you who have read this far. I wish that everyone can find something that you enjoy doing, and perhaps become your tiny little meaning and purpose of living (if you can't find any at first). ‚ù§

P.S. If you need someone to talk to, feel free to reach me via Discord or Instagram . I promise I will be a good listener.

Be kind to the world, because your single spark can start a praire fire.
